Preparing Your Home

Ensure a soft landing for your new ward with these essential guardian preparations.

flutter_dash

Quiet Acclimation

Position the sanctuary in a low-traffic area for the first 48 hours. Let the bird observe the household rhythm without direct interference.

Nutritional Welcome

Prepare fresh water and a curated seed-and-pellet mix as recommended in your guardian handbook. Hydration is critical post-flight.

Safe Stimulation

Introduce one or two non-toxic, wooden toys. Avoid overwhelming the space; gradual introduction of stimuli builds lasting trust.
