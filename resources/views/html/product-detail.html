Mastering the Art of Care

Maintaining a Hyacinth Macaw requires dedication and specific environmental parameters. Our specialists have curated this elite guardianship guide.

home_health

Habitat Design

Requires an aviary of at least 12x12x20ft. Structural integrity must withstand high bite pressure. Hardwood perches of varying diameters are essential.

  • Powder-coated stainless steel
  • Natural light integration
restaurant

Artisanal Nutrition

Unlike other macaws, Hyacinths require a high-fat diet consisting primarily of macadamia, palm nuts, and brazil nuts to thrive.

nutrition
diversity_3

Social Interaction

Highly intelligent and emotional. Requires minimum 4-6 hours of direct interaction daily to prevent psychological distress.
